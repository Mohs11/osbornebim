BIM Levels

Here you’ll find information on BIM levels according to Osborne. In short they are:

  • Simple
  • Moderate
  • Complex

These are the categories of BIM projects that we are able to implement.

Read on to find out more about each one.

Simple

This is the first level - the simplest package of BIM we offer.

Standards, Methods and Procedures

Feature

Electronic Data Capture Within a CDE

Single Source of Information

Trendable, Reusable Consistent Data

Greater / Richer Data Associated with information being captured

Benefit

Increased Site Efficiency

Speed of Information Recovery / Accuracy

Draw conclusion’s over multiple projects and understand the impact of decisions made

Easier to Find addtional information in the Future

Moderate

This is the second level - BIM with 3D models and more tools.

Standards, Methods and Procedures

Feature

3D Model

Visualisations

3D Data Capture

Volumes / Progress Tracking

Benefit

Stakeholder Engagement

Workforce Briefing to Aid Understanding Leading to Better Safety / Quality

Robust Planning of Work

30 - Easier Storage + Recovery of Element Data

Better Structured Data

More Informed Forecasts and Progress Updates

Complex

This is the third level - 3D analysis and all the data.

Standards, Methods and Procedures

Feature

Additional Information - Time | Cost | Quality | Carbon

Asset Information Model

Clash Detection

Reduced Rework

Benefit

Better Design Decisions

Better Value to Employer

Holistic - Better Integrated Decisions

Opex Reduced via Handover of 3D Model

Future Works Have Better Access to Historic Data

FAQ's

A collection of Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I want to implement some BIM tools on my project, how do I get started?

A. If you've picked out the tools you want then contact one of us and we'll get you set up. We're going to need to know:

  • Your Project Scope
  • Your Project Name
  • The Tools You Want

Q. I've read everything, but I still don't get it, can someone help me?

A. That's ok, don't worry. Let one of us know and we'll be happy to explain what you don't understand.

Q. There's a term or acronym that I don't understand, how do I find out what it means?

A. If it's not in our Glossary then contact us and not only will we help you to understand what it is but we'll add it to the list.

Q. Is BIM going to mean that I lose my job?

A. Not at all, BIM doesn't replace you, it just aids you. If anything it'll mean that you can do your job better and quicker, meaning that we can take on more work.

Q. Where does all the information go?

A. All the data that you upload is stored on an online server (the cloud). Its secure and safe and is only shared with the people who have been added to the project. This means you can access it from anywhere, all you need is a browser like Internet Explorer.

Q. I don't think this affects me, does it?

A. BIM affects everyone, it doesn't matter what your role, some aspect of BIM will make your job easier to do. This could be by providing you with an easier way to access information, such as photos. Just ask yourself this, how many times have you had to email someone, call someone or look through lots of documents to find information?.. Well with BIM that information will be there faster and in a much better format. Now that's surely a positive effect.

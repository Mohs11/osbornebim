Why BIM?

Working in an BIM enviroment provides the opportunity to make the job you do now easier and faster. Information is saved, shared and found faster which makes life a lot more simple.

This process aids to ease your daily working life. Replacing the legwork with one simple digital process (usually a tap of a button)

We have already made efficiencies on other projects and aim to make the same savings across the company.

That's the bottom line, it makes our job easier. We are able to capture and manipulate data more accurately and faster.

As you've probably also heard, there is a government mandate when considering BIM. If you would like more information on that then please follow this link. But the mandate isn't why we are doing this, we're doing it because it's the future and it makes sense.

The way we live at home and outside of work have changed so much over the past 10 years, I can pretty much bet that you have a smart phone near you right now and that to some extent that has improved your personal life. Why not use all these technologies to improve the way we work? To find out more about what technologies we're talking about, head over to the tools
